Jamestown Ship!

You have chosen the Jamestown ship! Good choice!

Your ship has around 100 people on it and so far things seem to be ok.

A group of people began to start having weird symptoms, like coughing up blood and constantly vomiting. After a week, the group died.

Your ship is asking your opinion on the situation. Do you:

Try to find the cause of the symptoms to stop further infection
Carefully throw them off the ship
