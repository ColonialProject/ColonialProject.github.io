Your colony has met a complex problem.

Many people in your colony are suffering an unknown disease or sickness.

This sickness is causing terrible symptoms for it's victims, such as:

  • Becoming less conscious
  • Thirst
  • Fatigue

After a while, people begin to die from this in your colony.



Information we know about it now: We know that the issue they were having was Hypernatremia (saltwater poisoning), which means that they weren't drinking enough water compared to salt, but the issue was that the water was extremely salty, and drinking the water just made it worst.
